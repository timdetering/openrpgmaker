The tiling mode allows one to fine tune the tiling direction of any block tiles and animated block tiles that are present in the current map. To modify the tiling direction of a tile simply click on any of the eight arrows surround the edges of the tile, or click on the 'A' in the center to autocalculate the tiling for that individual tile. The eight arrows all point away from the tile's center and indicates whether or not a tile tiles in that direction. A solid green arrow means the tile does tile in that direction, while an outline of an arrow indicates that it does not. The following images show which arrow modifies which part of the tile.

NOTE: In tiling mode the zoom buttons are disabled, and the map view is at a constant 3:2 zoom ratio (tiles are 48 pixels in size as opposed to 32 pixels for a 1:1 zoom).
